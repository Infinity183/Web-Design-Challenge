Figure 2: Latitude vs. Humidity


This scatter plot gauges the relationship between the recorded humidity of each city and that city's latitude. The statistics here imply there is no significant correlation whatsoever between city latitude and humidity, as the overall humidity percentages do not skew higher and lower depending on the latitude.
